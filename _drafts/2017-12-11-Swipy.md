Swipy
==============

Swipy &copy; is an e-reader tool which customers will purchase in order to read their favorite books hands-free. It has been a common issue for people to find the "sweet spot" while reading in bed or on the couch. One hand must awkwardly remain extended in order to hold the e-reader and turn pages. This device is intended for e-readers, but can also be used for any tablet of any size.
